Why eXploropia?


eXploropia is an organized tour company in Ethiopia established in 2019. Its major goal is to develop
different forms of tourism with a new and improved quality of service provisions. We provide quality
services and operate on various trips in a responsible way. And Our aim is to provide excellent service
and unforgettable memories for each and every client. We sincerely hope that they will tell everyone about
Ethiopia and the quality time spent with us.
Our tours emphasize the living culture, nature and history of the country. We believe that spending time with local people in their homelands gives a true picture of Ethiopia and a unique experience for travelers.



Our Guides


We assign multi-lingual trained professionals accordingly to client's language preferences. We have professional
guides who speak English, Italy, Germen, French, Russia and Spanish. In addition, we use local guides at all historic
sites and have responsible agents at all attractions that care for our clients on behalf of eXploropia.



Our Vehicles and Drivers


Our Vehicles are comprehensively insured, conditioned with Ac, equipped with refrigerator and we use different cars
for different sites. our offer for Addis city tour, historic north and Harrar high bus, coaster Bus , and mini-van
based on the interest and number of clients. 4WD Toyota Land cruiser or Nissan [4x4] based on models will be offer
for Omo Valley, Danakil Depression and other remote area.
Our drivers are geared with travel etiquette, knows places very well, safely drive, and fairly speak English.
